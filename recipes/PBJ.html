Peanut butter and jelly

Description

A delicious, healthy snack that is easy to prepare and easier to consume!

Ingredients

  • 2 slice of bread (dealer's choice)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter (32 grams)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of jam (30 grams)

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one side of the first slice of bread
  2. Spread jam on one side of the other slice of bread
  3. Place the bread, peanut butter side down, on top of the jam side of the other piece of bread
  4. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally for best results
