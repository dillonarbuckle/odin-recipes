Buffalo Dip Chicken

Description

A spicy, creamy chicken dish that tastes like a dip!

Ingredients

  • 2 spounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup (or more) of buffalo sauce
  • 1 (8oz) block of cream cheese
  • Chips or celery, for dipping (optional)

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into a crock pot
  2. Pour buffalo sauce over the chicken breasts to cover them
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours
  4. Add the block of cream cheese on top and cook for an additional 15 minutes
  5. Shred chicken while mixing in the cream cheese
  6. Serve on its own, over rice, with chips, or celery sticks!
